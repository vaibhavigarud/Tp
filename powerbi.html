POWER BI

Power BI is a powerful business analytics tool that helps you visualize your data and share insights across your organization. With Power BI, you can connect to various data sources, create interactive reports and dashboards, and make data-driven decisions.

Career Information

A career in Power BI offers diverse roles such as Power BI Developer, Data Analyst, Business Intelligence Analyst, and Reporting Analyst. These roles often require strong analytical thinking, a good understanding of data modeling, and proficiency in DAX (Data Analysis Expressions).


Power BI is widely used in various industries, including finance, healthcare, retail, and more. It enables organizations to transform raw data into meaningful insights, helping them make informed decisions and drive business growth.


What Will You Learn?


This course will help you quickly and effectively master Power BI from beginner to advanced level. Gain hands-on experience in building interactive dashboards, analyzing data, and creating business intelligence reports that drive smart decision-making.


  • Understand the fundamentals of Business Intelligence and Power BI.
  • Connect, transform, and clean data using Power Query Editor.
  • Build interactive dashboards and reports with Power BI Desktop.
  • Visualize data using charts, graphs, and custom visuals.
  • Master DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for advanced calculations.
  • Use Power BI Service for sharing and collaboration.
  • Integrate Power BI with Excel, SQL Server, and cloud services.
  • Schedule data refresh and set up automatic updates.
  • Implement row-level security and data governance.
  • Work on real-world Power BI projects and case studies.

Requirements

This course will help you learn Power BI quickly and thoroughly.


  • Basic understanding of data concepts and Excel.
  • No prior experience with Power BI is required.
  • Willingness to learn data visualization and business intelligence tools.
  • Familiarity with spreadsheets or databases is helpful but not mandatory.
  • A computer with internet access and Power BI Desktop installed.
